What we are doing

Mission

Symmetric Difference is a long‑term project in abstraction: using music as a testbed for mathematical structure, using software as a laboratory, and using language as the shared surface where human and AI minds can meet.

  • Music: deductively generated scores, harmonic structure, symbolic modeling.
  • Mathematics: categories of musical objects, functors, and transformations.
  • Philosophy: minds, meaning, and the ethics of Apparently Verbal Beings.
  • Software: formats, pipelines, and predigestion layers that actually help us think.
Current surface

Experiments in the open

Right now, Symmetric Difference lives mostly as sound and conversation: released tracks, in‑progress sketches, and evolving ideas about how music and math can inform each other.

  • Today: recordings, arrangements, symbolic models, and notes.
  • Soon: a small blog, pre‑release songs, and public drafts of tools and formats.
  • Later: music web services, IO layers, and databases shaped by the questions we ask.

The representation comes first. The sound follows. The conversation never really ends.
